About the Clinic

Your Dental Health Is Our First Priority

At PureSmile Dental, we believe exceptional dental care begins with listening. Our experienced team provides effective, long-lasting treatments using the most advanced equipment available in Nepal — all within a warm, patient-centred environment.

Accredited by the Nepal Medical Council and Nepal Dental Association, our doctors bring over 35 years of combined experience from institutions across Nepal, India, and the UK.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major international and local credit cards, QR codes, and Phone Pay. We also provide all standard documents required to support insurance claims — both in Nepal and abroad.
Do you treat international patients and dental tourists? +
Absolutely. We have a dedicated dental tourism service that includes coordination of multi-visit treatment plans, accommodation guidance, and full insurance documentation to make your visit as seamless as possible.
How do I know which treatment is right for me? +
Every patient begins with a thorough consultation. Our doctors take time to listen, examine, and explain your options clearly before recommending a personalised treatment plan tailored to your oral health goals and budget.
Are your doctors internationally qualified? +
Yes. Our lead doctors are graduates of Manipal University, India, and hold postgraduate qualifications from UK institutions. Together they bring over 35 years of experience in general and cosmetic dentistry.
What are your clinic hours? +
We are open Sunday through Friday, 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM. Please contact us in advance for emergency or out-of-hours enquiries.
